01 · Hook (0:00 - 0:20)

Open with the $1,200 jacket. No intro, no "hey guys." Straight into the flex.

02 · Problem / agitation (0:20 - 1:40)

Name the real problem. It's not the follower count. It's the pitch. Land the feeling: "You keep posting, brands keep ignoring you, and you don't know if it's the content or the approach."

03 · Solution / mechanism (1:40 - 3:00)

Introduce the three-part pitch: shot list, price anchor, delivery date. Reframe niche from a limit to a specialty brands want.

04 · Credibility (3:00 - 4:15)

Your own track record. Your feed as public proof. Student wins (real or placeholder). Anti-guru beat.

05 · Offer (4:15 - 6:30)

Course, bonuses, community (if applicable), price, guarantee. Frame price against a single paid deal.

06 · Close (6:30 - 8:00)

Scarcity-free CTA. "Every week you don't pitch is a week a brand doesn't know you exist."
