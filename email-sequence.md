## Sending notes

- **Reply handling:** Any reply at any point stops the sequence. Move them to a 1:1 thread.
- **Subject line A/B:** Emails 1 and 5 are the highest-impact. A/B "I built something for [BUSINESS NAME]" vs "Your Stan Store is doing the wrong job" on email 1.
- **Deliverability:** Send from a warmed inbox on a domain that isn't brand new. Plain text only. No tracking pixels (they tank reply rate on cold creator-economy audiences).
- **From name:** "Henryque Roa" (or "Henryque at Zytrom"). Real name beats brand name for open rate on cold outreach.
- **IG DM pairing:** Send a one-liner IG DM on Day 2 and Day 12 with the hub link. IG + email combo beats either alone for creators.
